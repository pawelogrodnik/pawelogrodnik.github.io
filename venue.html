Practical information

Attendance is limited to 20 participants (first come first served basis).

Registration fee

Registration fee is 500 EUR for ESSO-members and 650 EUR for non-members. The fee includes registration to the course, course materials (including positions’ papers for further reading), coffee breaks, lunches and social dinner.

Registration fee for the hands-on workshop on Transanal Endoscopic Operations in the elderly is only 50 EUR for the participants of the main course (including lunch).

To register in this course visit our site at:
http://www.essoweb.org/courses/esso-course-surgical-geriatric-oncology/
